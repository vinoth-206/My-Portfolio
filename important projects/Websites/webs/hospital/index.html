About Apollo Hospitals

Founded with a mission to improve health outcomes and access to specialised care, Apollo Hospitals combines clinical excellence, research and community programs.

Mission & Values

Delivering evidence-based, patient-centred care. Core values: Compassion, Integrity, Excellence, Teamwork.

Accreditations

NABH accredited, ISO 9001:2015. Continuous quality and infection control programs across services.

Community Programs

Outreach screening camps, telehealth for rural clinics and health education initiatives.

Departments

Comprehensive specialty and subspecialty services, staffed by experienced multidisciplinary teams.

Cardiology

Interventional cardiology, cath labs, heart failure programmes and cardiac rehabilitation.

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Stroke unit, neurocritical care, epilepsy services and advanced neuroimaging.

Orthopedics

Joint replacement, arthroscopy, trauma and physiotherapy-led recovery programs.

Oncology

Medical, surgical and radiation oncology, multidisciplinary tumor boards and palliative care.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

High-risk pregnancy clinic, neonatology and minimally invasive gynecologic surgery.

Emergency & Critical Care

24/7 emergency services, trauma team and multi-disciplinary ICUs.

Clinical & Diagnostic Services

State-of-the-art imaging, labs and allied health services supporting patient care and clinical decision making.

Imaging & Laboratory

CT (128-slice), MRI 3T, PET-CT, advanced ultrasound and a 24/7 accredited lab (biochemistry, hematology, microbiology, pathology).

  • Rapid turnaround for critical tests
  • Telepathology & remote reporting
  • Quality-controlled reference lab partnerships

Rehabilitation & Allied Health

Physiotherapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, clinical nutrition and chronic pain management.

Patient Support Services

Case management, social work, interpreter services, insurance support and pastoral care. Detailed pre-op counselling and discharge planning ensure continuity of care.
